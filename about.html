About

Tic Tac Toe 5×5 is a modern twist on the classic game, designed specifically for Indian players with a warm, vibrant theme inspired by India's rich culture.

Developed by Pritam Dey in 2025, this game aims to bring friends and family together through fun and challenging gameplay.

Enjoy a beautiful, easy-to-use interface with a touch of Indian colors and fonts. Thank you for playing!
